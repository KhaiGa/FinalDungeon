About this project


Final Dungeon is a free upcoming 2D Top Down Dungeon Crawler Game developed using Unity.
The game is set in an underground world with a variety of dungeons to "crawl" your way through. By means such as a steep grinding system, unforgiving deaths and seemingly never-ending dungeons with increasing difficulty it hopes to keep even the most experienced players on the edge of their seats.



Gameplay

Light Attack

Deal minor damage in a narrow arc in direction of the cursor.


Charge Attack

Jump backwards and enter a charging form. Upon releasing quickly dash forward and deal high damage in a wide arc in front of you.

Three Charging States


Lesser Charge: Deal increased damage
High Charge: Deal higher damage and briefly stun enemies
Highest Charge: Deal maximum damage and stun enemies for a longer period of time

Releasing during the Perfect Attack Window (dark red indicator following each state) will enhance damage and stun duration


Shield Block

Block incoming attacks from the front. Blocking with the right timing will result in a Perfect Block.


Perfect Block(Melee): Briefly stun enemies and knock them back
Perfect Block(Projectile): Reflect the projectile





Enemies

Slimes

The simplest of enemies, coming in various colors and sizes.
Despite their appearance, in numbers they can pose a threat not to be taken lightly

Wizard


Wizards are ancient protectors of dungeons and the secrets within. They wear long violet robes and carry a magic staff with which they cast devastating magical attacks on their enemies.

Shadow

Shadow creatures copy not only the appearance but also techniques and strengths of their enemy. According to rumors they were created by mages of old to guard kingdoms long fallen.

Enhancement



Enemies can drop Gems of Level 1. Each Gem has both a positive and a negative effect. Combining Gems of the same Level creates a Gem of the next higher Level that has a stronger positive and a weaker negative effect. The maximum Level is 3. The Player can only equip 3 Gems at the same time.
